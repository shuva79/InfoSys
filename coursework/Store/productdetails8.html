Japanese Juniper

RS.900

The Japanese Juniper is a hardy evergreen, found in temperate regions of the world. Junipers generally have a naturally flowing or "cascading" shape of long, trailing branches, and can be trained into many amazing Bonsai shapes. The branches usually respond well to wiring and reshaping.

Botanical Name: Juniperus Procumbens nana

Classification: Conifer

Foliage colour: Green (Spring-Summer) Brownish Green (Autumn-Winter)

Origin: Japan

Approx Age: 8+ Years (Approximately)

Size: 13cm H & 7cm W (with pot)
